# Privacy Policy for LockTracker

**Last Updated: January 2025**

## Overview

LockTracker ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our browser extension and web application.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information
- Email address (for account creation and login)
- Password (securely hashed, never stored in plain text)

### Betting Data
When you use the "Sync" feature, we collect betting information from supported sportsbooks, including:
- Bet descriptions and matchups
- Wager amounts
- Odds
- Results (win/loss/pending)
- Profit/loss amounts

This data is only collected when you explicitly click "Sync Now" or have auto-sync enabled (Pro feature).

### Technical Information
- Browser type and version
- Extension usage statistics (which features you use)

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Provide the LockTracker service (tracking and analyzing your bets)
- Calculate statistics (profit, ROI, win rate)
- Improve our service
- Send important account notifications

## Data Storage and Security

- Your data is stored securely using Supabase (PostgreSQL database)
- All data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS
- Passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms
- Each user can only access their own data

## Data Sharing

**We do NOT:**
- Sell your personal information
- Share your betting data with third parties
- Use your data for advertising purposes

**We may share data only:**
- When required by law
- To protect our rights or safety

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:
- **Supabase** - Database and authentication
- **Stripe** - Payment processing (for Pro subscriptions)
- **Railway** - Web hosting

These services have their own privacy policies.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your data
- Delete your account and all associated data
- Export your betting data
- Opt out of any marketing communications

To exercise these rights, contact us or use the account settings in the app.

## Data Retention

- Your data is retained as long as your account is active
- Upon account deletion, all your data is permanently removed
- Deleted data cannot be recovered

## Children's Privacy

LockTracker is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes via email or in-app notification.
