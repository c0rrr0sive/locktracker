## Short Description (132 characters max)

## Detailed Description
Stop guessing if you're actually profitable. LockTracker automatically syncs your bets from popular sportsbooks and shows you the stats that matter.

**Supported Platforms:**
- PrizePicks
- FanDuel Sportsbook
- DraftKings Sportsbook

**Features:**
- One-click sync from your bet history
- Track profit, win rate, and ROI
- Breakdown by sport and bet type
- See which bets are actually making you money
- Clean, dark-themed dashboard

**How It Works:**
1. Install the extension
2. Create a free account at LockTracker
3. Go to your sportsbook's bet history page
4. Click "Sync Now" to import your bets
5. View your stats on the LockTracker dashboard
