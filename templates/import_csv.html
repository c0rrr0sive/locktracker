CSV Format

Your CSV should have these columns (order doesn't matter):

Date,Sport,Matchup,Bet Type,Description,Odds,Amount,Result,Sportsbook

Example row:

2025-01-10,NBA,Lakers vs Celtics,Spread,Lakers -3.5,-110,100,win,FanDuel

Result should be: win, loss, push, or pending. Odds should be American format (e.g., -110, +150).
